About Us

TechTogether Naperville is a student-led nonprofit founded by Aryan Chhabra, a Neuqua Valley High School student. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide in our community through free, high-impact technology programs that empower both youth and seniors.

Our Programs

Code for Kids 204

A free after-school program that introduces elementary students to computer science using tools like Scratch and Code.org. Led by trained high school volunteers, this workshop fosters creativity, problem-solving, and early STEM interest.

TechHelp Teens

A community initiative where high school students provide tech assistance and basic digital literacy training for senior citizens. We help with smartphones, tablets, internet safety, and using everyday apps.
