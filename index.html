Connecting Generations Through Technology

About Us

TechTogether Naperville is a student-led nonprofit founded by Aryan Chhabra. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by offering free, community-focused technology programs that empower both youth and seniors to thrive in the digital world.

Our Programs

Code for Kids: Python Track

A free, 8-week coding program for middle school and early high school students, where participants learn Python through hands-on projects.

Fall 2025 Alive ATI Program:
Tuesdays, September 2 – October 21, 2025
Location: Alive Center, 500 W 5th Ave, Naperville, IL 60563

Programs in Development

Code for Kids: Scratch Track – A beginner-friendly 8-week coding series for elementary students.
TechHelp Teens – High school volunteers assist seniors with smartphones, apps, and safe internet use.

These programs are currently in the outreach stage, with future plans to expand to community partners like Fry Family YMCA.
